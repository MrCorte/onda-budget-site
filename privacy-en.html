Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data collection

Onda Budget does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered in the app (expenses, budgets, categories) is saved exclusively on the user's device.

Data saved locally

The app saves the following data on your device:

  • Expenses entered by the user
  • Categories and configured budgets
  • App preferences
  • Subscription status

This data never leaves the device without the user's explicit consent.

Camera and photos

Access to the camera and photo library is requested only for the receipt scanning feature. Images are not transmitted to external servers without explicit consent.

Face ID / Touch ID

Biometric authentication is optional and managed entirely by iOS. Onda Budget has no access to the user's biometric data.

Subscriptions

In-app purchases are managed through the Apple App Store. Andrea Cortesi has no access to payment data. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Notifications

Local notifications (budget alerts, recurring renewals) are generated directly on the device and do not involve any data transmission.
